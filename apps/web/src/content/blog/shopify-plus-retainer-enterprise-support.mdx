## What Makes Shopify Plus Different

### Checkout Extensibility

Plus gives you access to checkout customization through Checkout UI Extensions. This lets you add custom fields, upsells, loyalty point redemption, gift messages, and branded checkout experiences that standard Shopify stores can't touch.

But checkout customization is also the most sensitive area of your store. A bug in checkout directly costs you completed orders. This code needs careful development, thorough testing, and ongoing maintenance as Shopify evolves the checkout API.

### Shopify Scripts

Scripts let you create custom pricing logic, shipping rules, and payment method filtering. They run at the cart and checkout level, affecting every transaction. A broken Script can apply wrong discounts (costing you margin) or block valid payment methods (costing you sales).

Scripts require Ruby knowledge and deep understanding of Shopify's Script API. Most standard Shopify developers don't have this expertise.

### Shopify Flow

Flow automations handle critical business processes: order tagging, inventory alerts, fraud flagging, customer segmentation, and more. As your business grows, Flow automations become increasingly complex and interconnected.

Without dedicated management, Flow automations accumulate like code without tests. They work until something changes — a new product type, a modified fulfillment process, a third-party integration update — and then they fail silently, causing downstream problems you don't discover for days.

### Multi-Market and B2B

Plus supports multiple storefronts, B2B wholesale channels, and international markets with localized pricing and content. Each market adds complexity: different currencies, tax rules, shipping providers, payment methods, and compliance requirements.

Managing multi-market isn't a one-time setup. It's ongoing work as you expand to new regions, update pricing, and comply with changing regulations.

### Exclusive APIs and Integrations

Plus merchants get access to APIs that standard stores don't: the Multipass API for single sign-on, the Gift Card API, advanced discount APIs, and higher API rate limits. These integrations connect your store to ERP systems, CRM platforms, PIM tools, and warehouse management systems.

Each integration is a potential failure point that needs monitoring and maintenance.

## Red Flags in Your Current Plus Setup

If any of these describe your situation, you need enterprise-level support:

- **Your ERP integration breaks at least once a quarter** — and fixing it takes days
- **You launched a new market and still have unresolved tax or compliance issues**

- **Peak traffic causes performance issues** — despite Plus's higher resource allocation
