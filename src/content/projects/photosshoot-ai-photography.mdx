## Problem Statement

Product photography is one of the biggest bottlenecks for e-commerce brands. A single product shoot costs $500-2,000, takes days to schedule, and weeks to deliver. For brands with hundreds of SKUs, this becomes a six-figure annual expense.

Small and medium e-commerce brands — especially Shopify merchants — often settle for amateur photos that directly hurt their conversion rates. They needed an affordable, fast alternative that still delivered professional results.

## Solution

We built PhotosShoot as an AI-powered platform that democratizes professional product photography:

### AI Photography Engine

- **Background Removal**: Automatic, clean background removal from product uploads
- **Scene Generation**: AI-generated lifestyle backgrounds matching brand aesthetics
- **Lighting Correction**: Automatic exposure, white balance, and shadow optimization
- **Batch Processing**: Process hundreds of product images in minutes, not weeks

### E-Commerce Integration

- **Shopify Plugin**: Direct sync with Shopify stores for product image management
- **Bulk Upload**: CSV and API-based bulk image processing for large catalogs
- **Format Optimization**: Auto-generated images in multiple sizes for web, social, and marketplace

### Platform Features

- **Brand Presets**: Save custom styles, backgrounds, and settings for consistent branding
- **Team Collaboration**: Multi-user access with approval workflows
- **Usage Dashboard**: Track credits, processing history, and download analytics
- **API Access**: RESTful API for custom integrations and automated workflows

### Technical Architecture

- **Frontend**: Next.js with React for a responsive image editing interface
- **AI Pipeline**: Python-based ML pipeline for image processing and generation
- **Infrastructure**: AWS for scalable compute and image storage
- **Payments**: Stripe for subscription and credit-based billing
- **CDN**: CloudFront for fast global image delivery

## Results

PhotosShoot launched as an accessible alternative to traditional product photography:

✅ **90% Cost Reduction** — Professional product images at a fraction of traditional shoot costs
✅ **Minutes, Not Weeks** — Images ready in minutes instead of weeks of waiting
✅ **Consistent Quality** — AI ensures uniform lighting and styling across entire catalogs
✅ **Shopify Integration** — Direct sync keeps product images updated automatically
